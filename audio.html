Live Presentation - Coursework - May 2021

This was the second piece of coursework I completed for my Audio module at University. This includes a scripted element, voxpops and an interview. I loved combining all the audio elements and putting together a final piece. The subject is based on Covid-19s effect on Pet Sales & rehoming.

Creating audio has been super interesting - especially learning all about the editing of volumes and how to properly record e.g. correct microphones, settings, sound booths...
