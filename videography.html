Videography may be my all time favourite form of media. Having complete creative freedom and forming stories out of clips of film is so fascinating to me. I love how someone can become encaptured in your work.

Be Safe, Be Seen - Coursework Piece for TECH1004 - 2021

This was an assignment set for my moving image module at University. We had the choice of either creating a public informational film or a short news package. I decided to create an informational film based on road safety - specifically at night. This was a super fun piece as I could be as creative as I'd like and had little restriction on the end product - as long as it related to the set task.
